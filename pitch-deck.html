02 / Problem

Legal teams drown in information, but cannot trust generic AI output.

Statutes, cases, templates, PDFs, policies, and matter facts sit in different places. Legal professionals need speed, but hallucinated or weak citations create unacceptable review risk.

03 / Solution

Retrieve broadly. Verify legal evidence. Control the final citation set.

1. Candidate retrieval

Generate multi-view queries and retrieve a broad evidence pool from legal corpora.

2. Selection bottleneck

Rank, compare, and verify citation candidates with legal-dimension checks.

3. Submission exactness

Normalize citations, validate vocabulary, and output only corpus-backed references.
